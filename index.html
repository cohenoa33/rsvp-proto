managing RSVPs easily

This application is designed to simplify the process of managing RSVPs for your events. Whether you're organizing a wedding, conference, party, or any other gathering, this app will streamline the RSVP process and help you keep track of your guest list effortlessly.

Invitations

Send out personalized invitations to your guests via email or SMS directly from the app. Customize the invitation message to suit the tone and style of your event.

RSVP Tracking

Keep track of responses from your guests in real-time. View who has accepted, declined, or not responded to your invitation.

Reporting

Generate detailed reports on RSVP status, guest attendance, meal preferences, and more. Export reports in various formats for easy sharing and analysis.
